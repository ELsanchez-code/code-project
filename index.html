Tournament Rules

  1. No cheating or use of third-party apps/hacks — immediate disqualification.
  2. Late more than 5 minutes = automatic ban for that match (forfeiture).
  3. Cannot change player after registration closes.
  4. Each player must be Mythic rank or above to be eligible.
  5. Teams must field 5 players per match. (5 active + 1 substitute allowed in registration.)
  6. Each player must have a designated role (Tank, Fighter, Assassin, Mage, Marksman, Support).
  7. Max teams: 50 capacity. Bracket/format will be announced based on registrations.
  8. Referee decisions are final.
